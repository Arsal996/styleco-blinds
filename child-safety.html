    Best time to replace

    The WCMA recommends replacing all window coverings made before 2001 with today’s safer products, and using cordless window coverings in children’s bedrooms and play areas. If replacement isn't an option, we can also properly install you FREE Child Safe Retrofit Kits, from the Window Covering Safety Council, that modify blind or shade cords to reduce the risk of strangulation.

    Cord blocks and and Safety Tensioners

    When there are little children in a house, one of the most recurring fears is associated with the cords that allow window blinds to move. A suitable addition to a product recently added is the installation of safety systems complementing the product, such as cord blocks and and safety tensioners.

    Cordless Blinds

    Getting rid of cord blinds, sounds easy right? Well, it is. With cordless blinds and shades, the dangers of cord blinds are gone.
